press i to enter INSERT mode
press esc to leave INSERT mode
press o to enter line below the cursor
press v to enter view mode and to highlight some text,
   then press y to confirm the selection,
   then press p key to copy the selection at a cursor's position
   press u to undo the paste 
press : to enter exit mode
	type w to write file (add space and name the file)
press shift+g to move cursor to the bottom of the file
press dw to delete word under the cursor 
press dd to delete the whole line under the cursor
press gg to go to the top of the file
press shift+a to append text to the end of the line
